Start Trading

Welcome to stockspectra! To start trading, please follow these steps:

  1. Create a new account or log in to your existing account.
  2. Deposit funds into your account.
  3. Choose the stock you want to trade and select the quantity.
  4. Confirm your trade and wait for the transaction to be processed.
